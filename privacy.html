Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

Giant Brand Company Ltd ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact or identity information you voluntarily provide when contacting us or placing an order.
  • Payment Information: Credit card numbers and related financial data when you make purchases (processed securely by our payment processor).
  • Derivative Data: Information our servers automatically collect when you access the site, such as your IP address, browser type, and access times.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

Process Orders

Fulfill your purchases and provide order confirmations

Customer Support

Respond to your inquiries and provide assistance

Improve Services

Enhance our website and service offerings

Marketing

Send promotional materials (if you opt-in)

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

Encryption

All sensitive data is encrypted during transmission

Access Control

Strict access limitations to personal data

Regular Audits

Ongoing security assessments and updates

Note: While we implement these security measures, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

Access

Request a copy of your personal data

Correction

Update or correct inaccurate information

Deletion

Request removal of your personal data

Objection

Object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.
